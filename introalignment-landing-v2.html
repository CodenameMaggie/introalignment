Questions You Might Have

How is this different from dating apps?

Dating apps optimize for engagement—keeping you swiping. We optimize for outcomes—helping you find the right person and then getting out of your way. We don't have endless profiles to browse. Instead, we take time to understand you deeply, then make thoughtful introductions to people who could genuinely be right for you.

What do you mean by "conversation"?

When you join IntroAlignment, you'll chat with our AI—but not like a customer service bot. It's a warm, natural conversation about your life, your experiences, what you value, and what you're looking for. Through your stories and reflections, we learn who you truly are. It typically takes about 30-40 minutes, and you can take breaks whenever you like.

How do you decide who to introduce me to?

We use a combination of validated psychological frameworks and time-tested wisdom traditions to understand compatibility at a deep level. We look at values alignment, life vision compatibility, emotional patterns, communication styles, and more. We only make an introduction when we see genuine potential for a meaningful connection.

Is this free?

We'll announce our pricing model closer to launch. We believe in making quality matchmaking accessible while ensuring we can provide the depth of service that makes IntroAlignment special. Waitlist members will receive early access and special founding member benefits.

What if I'm not ready to date right now?

That's perfectly okay. Join our waitlist and we'll keep you updated. When you're ready, we'll be here. There's no pressure, no expiring matches, no artificial urgency. Finding the right person is worth taking time for.

How do you protect my privacy?

Your privacy is sacred to us. Your conversations and personal information are encrypted and never shared with third parties. When we make an introduction, we only share what's necessary for you to decide if you'd like to connect. You're always in control of what's shared and when.
